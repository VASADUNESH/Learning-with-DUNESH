Operators

Operator is a special symbol designed for a particular task. C comes with 44 operators and 14 separators [, . : ; “ “ ‘ ‘ { },…. ] Operator works on operands.

Based on no of operands participating in operation, the operators divided into 3 types.

  1. Unary Operator:

    2. Require one operand.
    Eg: a++, a--, --a, ++a, sizeof(a), ~a, !a, +a, -a,…..

  2. Binary operator:

    3. Require two operands.
    Eg: a+b, a-b, a>b, a<=b, a!=b, a<>b,….

  3. Ternary operator:

    4. Require three expressions / operands.
    Eg: conditional part ? true part : false part;

Based on operation the operators divided into several types.

1.Assignment operator [ = ]:
  • It copies the value on its right side into the variable on its left side. In assignment operation, the left side operand should be a variable. i.e. expressions not allowed on left side.

    c=’X’;
